History


Documents versions are in form major.minor.build.patch.

major Changes when global changes are applied to project.
minor Is odd in non-stable versions (which has untested additions or extensions),
becomes even after testing these features.
build Increased with each rebuild. Set to zero when "major" changes.
patch this is level of completeness. May be "alpha" (a), "beta" (b), "release" (no letter), "fix/patch" (p).
